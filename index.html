Welcome to IIT Bombay's Human Motor Neurophysiology and Neuromodulation lab, a collaborative hub for graduate and undergraduate students driving innovative research in neurology. Our interests span motor neurophysiology, neuromodulation, and brain stimulation, covering theoretical insights to practical applications. We utilize EEG, EMG, brain stimulation, and neuroimaging techniques for three-dimensional simulations of neural dynamics, engaging in projects such as deciphering motor control and devising neuromodulation strategies. Backed by prestigious Indian neurological programs, we invite skilled and motivated students to join our ranks, participate in ongoing projects, and contribute to groundbreaking progress in neural engineering.
