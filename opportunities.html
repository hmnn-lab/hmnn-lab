The lab has openings for project assistants, summer interns and women scientists (WOS-A). Interested candidates may contact Dr. Nivethida (dr.nivethida[at]gmail[dot]com) with their CV and letter of interest through email. Candidates for summer internship should apply through Indian Academy of Sciences or IIT IRCC program and indicate your lab choice. Those interested to pursue PhD or MTech/MSc should apply formally through the procedure given by the Department of Biosciences and Bioengineering, IIT Bombay.
